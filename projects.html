Project deep dive: StellarNotes

Problem: Teams need a privacy-focused, real-time editor that works offline and syncs seamlessly. Solution: a CRDT-based document model with background sync, operational transforms, and encryption at-rest and in-transit.

Role & impact

  • Built the collaborative engine and sync layer; reduced merge conflicts by 92% versus naive approaches.
  • Designed the offline-first UX so changes survive intermittent connectivity.

Key design decisions

  1. CRDTs for local-first consistency
  2. WebRTC for peer-to-peer sync when possible
  3. IndexedDB for local persistence
